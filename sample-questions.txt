## Mathematics Questions

Q: What is 15 + 27?
A) 40
B) 41
C) 42
D) 43
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 15 plus 27 equals 42.

Q: What is the square root of 144?
A) 10
B) 11
C) 12
D) 13
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 12 multiplied by 12 equals 144.

Q: If x + 5 = 12, what is the value of x?
A) 5
B) 6
C) 7
D) 8
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Subtracting 5 from both sides: x = 12 - 5 = 7.

Q: What is 25% of 200?
A) 25
B) 50
C) 75
D) 100
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: 25% of 200 = 0.25 × 200 = 50.

Q: What is the next number in the sequence: 2, 4, 8, 16, ?
A) 24
B) 28
C) 32
D) 36
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Each number is multiplied by 2. So 16 × 2 = 32.

Q: What is the area of a rectangle with length 8cm and width 5cm?
A) 13 cm²
B) 26 cm²
C) 40 cm²
D) 80 cm²
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Area = length × width = 8 × 5 = 40 cm².

Q: What is 7 × 8?
A) 54
B) 56
C) 64
D) 72
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: 7 multiplied by 8 equals 56.

Q: What is the value of 3² + 4²?
A) 7
B) 12
C) 25
D) 49
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 3² = 9, 4² = 16, so 9 + 16 = 25.

Q: If a triangle has angles of 60°, 70°, and x°, what is x?
A) 40°
B) 45°
C) 50°
D) 55°
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Sum of angles in a triangle is 180°. So x = 180 - 60 - 70 = 50°.

Q: What is 1000 ÷ 25?
A) 25
B) 30
C) 35
D) 40
ANSWER: D
EXPLANATION: 1000 divided by 25 equals 40.

Q: What is the perimeter of a square with side length 6cm?
A) 12 cm
B) 18 cm
C) 24 cm
D) 36 cm
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Perimeter of a square = 4 × side = 4 × 6 = 24 cm.

Q: What is 3/4 as a percentage?
A) 60%
B) 70%
C) 75%
D) 80%
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 3/4 = 0.75 = 75%.

Q: What is the value of 2³?
A) 6
B) 8
C) 9
D) 10
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: 2³ = 2 × 2 × 2 = 8.

Q: What is the LCM of 4 and 6?
A) 10
B) 12
C) 18
D) 24
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: The least common multiple of 4 and 6 is 12.

Q: What is 0.5 + 0.25?
A) 0.5
B) 0.65
C) 0.75
D) 1.0
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 0.5 plus 0.25 equals 0.75.

Q: If y = 2x + 3 and x = 4, what is y?
A) 9
B) 10
C) 11
D) 12
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: y = 2(4) + 3 = 8 + 3 = 11.

Q: What is the HCF of 12 and 18?
A) 2
B) 3
C) 6
D) 9
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: The highest common factor of 12 and 18 is 6.

Q: What is 15% of 80?
A) 10
B) 11
C) 12
D) 13
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 15% of 80 = 0.15 × 80 = 12.

Q: What is the circumference of a circle with radius 7cm? (Use π = 22/7)
A) 22 cm
B) 44 cm
C) 88 cm
D) 154 cm
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: Circumference = 2πr = 2 × (22/7) × 7 = 44 cm.

Q: What is 99 - 37?
A) 52
B) 56
C) 62
D) 66
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 99 minus 37 equals 62.

Q: What is the value of |−8|?
A) -8
B) 0
C) 8
D) 16
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: The absolute value of -8 is 8.

Q: What is 12 × 12?
A) 124
B) 134
C) 144
D) 154
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 12 multiplied by 12 equals 144.

Q: What is the sum of the first 5 prime numbers?
A) 26
B) 28
C) 30
D) 32
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: The first 5 primes are 2, 3, 5, 7, 11. Sum = 2+3+5+7+11 = 28.

Q: What is 5! (5 factorial)?
A) 100
B) 110
C) 120
D) 130
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120.

Q: What is the slope of a line passing through (0,0) and (2,4)?
A) 1
B) 2
C) 3
D) 4
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: Slope = (y₂-y₁)/(x₂-x₁) = (4-0)/(2-0) = 4/2 = 2.

Q: What is 3/5 + 1/5?
A) 3/10
B) 2/5
C) 3/5
D) 4/5
ANSWER: D
EXPLANATION: When adding fractions with the same denominator: 3/5 + 1/5 = 4/5.

Q: What is the volume of a cube with side length 3cm?
A) 9 cm³
B) 18 cm³
C) 27 cm³
D) 36 cm³
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Volume of cube = side³ = 3³ = 27 cm³.

Q: What is 8 ÷ 0.4?
A) 2
B) 10
C) 20
D) 40
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 8 ÷ 0.4 = 8 ÷ (4/10) = 8 × (10/4) = 20.

Q: What is the median of: 3, 7, 9, 12, 15?
A) 7
B) 9
C) 10
D) 12
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: The median is the middle value when arranged in order, which is 9.

Q: What is 2x + 5x?
A) 3x
B) 7x
C) 10x
D) 7x²
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: Combining like terms: 2x + 5x = 7x.

Q: What is the area of a circle with radius 7cm? (Use π = 22/7)
A) 44 cm²
B) 88 cm²
C) 132 cm²
D) 154 cm²
ANSWER: D
EXPLANATION: Area = πr² = (22/7) × 7² = (22/7) × 49 = 154 cm².

Q: If 3x = 15, what is x?
A) 3
B) 4
C) 5
D) 6
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Dividing both sides by 3: x = 15 ÷ 3 = 5.

Q: What is 45% as a decimal?
A) 0.045
B) 0.45
C) 4.5
D) 45
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: 45% = 45/100 = 0.45.

Q: What is the mode of: 2, 5, 5, 7, 9?
A) 2
B) 5
C) 7
D) 9
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: The mode is the most frequently occurring value, which is 5.

Q: What is √64 + √36?
A) 10
B) 12
C) 14
D) 16
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: √64 = 8 and √36 = 6, so 8 + 6 = 14.

Q: What is the simple interest on $1000 at 5% per annum for 2 years?
A) $50
B) $75
C) $100
D) $150
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Simple Interest = (P × R × T)/100 = (1000 × 5 × 2)/100 = $100.

Q: What is 2/3 of 60?
A) 20
B) 30
C) 40
D) 50
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 2/3 of 60 = (2/3) × 60 = 40.

Q: What is the sum of interior angles in a pentagon?
A) 360°
B) 450°
C) 540°
D) 720°
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Sum = (n-2) × 180° = (5-2) × 180° = 540°.

Q: What is 10² - 8²?
A) 24
B) 32
C) 36
D) 64
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 10² = 100, 8² = 64, so 100 - 64 = 36.

Q: What is the ratio 20:30 in simplest form?
A) 1:2
B) 2:3
C) 3:4
D) 4:5
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: Dividing both by 10: 20:30 = 2:3.

Q: What is 0.8 × 5?
A) 2
B) 3
C) 4
D) 5
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 0.8 multiplied by 5 equals 4.
